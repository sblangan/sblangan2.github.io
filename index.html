My Favorite Things of the Moment

A listing of what I'm currently obsessed with

Summer is a great time to enjoy the little things. Here's the Top 3 things that have been bringing me more joyh in the everyday!

Number 1: Enneagrams

The Enneagram of Personality typifies individuals into 9 "types". Are you a "Helper", an "Achiever", an "Individualist"? Personally, I'm a 7--the Enthusiast. Everyone should take the time to find their personality type. It's revealing and insightful. Take the test here.

Number 2: Soccer

The Women's World Cup captured my attention far more that I thought it would. Now I've found myself trying to learn everything about this beautiful game! The best resource on the web to get soccer news is Feedspot, a comprehensive news feed that pulls soccer news from the best soccer resources online. Give it a try!

Number 3: French Macarons

Is there anything more quintessentially French? These old school desserts are getting clever facelifts thanks to some of the world's top macaron makers. My personal favorite is Makarohn, a Miami-based bakery with fierce creativity. Seriously, order their Harry Potter collection. They ship!
